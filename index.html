Features

Core tools for clean operations — payments, leases, screening, and maintenance.

Lease management

Keep leases organized with clear terms, renewal workflows, and easy access for both parties.

Tenant screening

Standardize applications and screening steps so decisions stay consistent and documented.

Maintenance tracking

Requests, updates, and vendor assignment — all tracked in one place with a clear timeline.
