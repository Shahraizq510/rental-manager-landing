How it works

1) Invite tenants

Add a unit and send an invite. Tenants get a simple pay-rent flow.

2) Tenants pay via ACH

ACH inside the app (Stripe + Plaid). Clean tracking, fewer fees than cards.

3) You get payouts + records

Payouts land in your bank account, with a tidy ledger for taxes and reporting.

Built for landlords & tenants

For landlords

  • Rent collection & ledger history
  • Lease tracking (start/end, rent, deposits)
  • Automated receipts & reminders
  • Simple dashboard for units & tenants

For tenants

  • Pay rent via bank transfer (ACH)
